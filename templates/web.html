Honors General Physics I

About the Course

Honors General Physics I is an introductory course designed to provide students with a solid foundation in classical mechanics. Topics covered in this course include kinematics, dynamics, Newton's laws of motion, work, energy, momentum, rotational motion, oscillations, and gravitation.
Emphasis is placed on problem-solving skills and understanding the fundamental principles underlying physical phenomena.

Course Objectives

  • Understand and apply the basic principles of classical mechanics.
  • Develop problem-solving skills in physics.
  • Apply mathematical concepts to analyze physical phenomena.
  • Develop critical thinking and analytical skills.

Prerequisites

Prerequisites for this course may include a strong background in algebra, trigonometry, and calculus. Students are expected to have a basic understanding of fundamental concepts in mathematics and physics.
